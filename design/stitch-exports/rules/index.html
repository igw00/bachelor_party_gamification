Official Rulebook

Edition 2024.2 • St. Petersburg, FL

01

Overview

expand_more

The St. Pete Invitational is a multi-disciplinary competitive event designed to test endurance, strategy, and social wit. Participants compete across three days of intense activity.

The primary goal is the accumulation of "Invitational Points" through a combination of physical challenges, tactical voting, and luck-based card draws. The competition maintains a strict code of conduct emphasizing sportsmanship and gentlemanly behavior.

"Victory belongs to the most persevering, but glory belongs to the most gracious."
02

Prize Pool

expand_more

The 2024 Purse is set at a total of $2,000 USD, funded through participant entry fees and local sponsorship. Distribution is as follows:

  • check_circle
    Grand Champion: 60% of total purse ($1,200), the Invitational Trophy, and naming rights for next year's opening challenge.
  • check_circle
    Runner Up: 30% of total purse ($600).
  • check_circle
    Third Place: 10% of total purse ($200).
03

Gameplay

expand_more

Phase A: The Morning Scramble

Contestants must check-in via the app by 08:00 EST. Failure to check-in results in a 10-point penalty. The first challenge is always an endurance-based movement task within the St. Petersburg downtown corridor.

Scoring Metric

Time + Accuracy

Gear Required

Invitational Kit

Phase B: Tactical Deliberation

Afternoon sessions focus on seated strategy. Participants will use the "Add" function in the app to submit scores from mini-games. All submissions must be verified by at least one other participant (The Peer-Review Rule).

warning Disqualification Clause

Any participant caught manipulating score entry or bypass the Peer-Review verification will be immediately relegated to the 'Spectator' class for the remainder of the Invitational.
